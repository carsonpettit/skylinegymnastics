Class Descriptions

Several different classes are available depending on skill levels. We offer Beginner classes up to Advanced gymnastics classes. We also offer classes in Tumbling only.

This program is designed to help develop you and your child’s awareness of their body and how it can move. It is also focused on developing their self-esteem and decision-making skills. During this class, a parent stays with the child through navigating equipment.

This program is an extension of Parents & Tots where they will additionally exercise the following skills:

  • Learn and develop problem-solving skills
  • Attend to a task from beginning to end
  • Further develop listening skills and language abilities
  • A sense of mastery and self-esteem

This program is designed to help develop children’s awareness of their body and basic gymnastic skills. Self-esteem and decision-making skills are also important.

Class Requirement:

No Skills

Children:

5-12 years old

Class Format:
  • Welcome at door – receive a group number
  • Meet in gymnasium
  • Warm-Ups/Basics/Stretching
  • Rotations through stations (8 min)
  • Culminating Activity
Events Covered:
  • Floor Exercise (Basic Skills)
  • Uneven Bars
  • Low Beam
  • High Beam
  • Vaulting
  • Trampoline
  • Double Mini
  • Rope Swing

This program helps beginner and intermediate gymnasts become more efficient in learning skills with understanding. Strength, agility, and flexibility are included.

Class Requirement:

Good cartwheel and round-off

Children:

7-13 years old (grouped by age/skill)

Class Format:
  • Welcome - receive a group number
  • Meet in gymnasium
  • Warm-Ups/Basics/Stretching
  • Rotations through stations (10-12 min)
  • Culminating Activity
Events Covered:
  • Floor Exercise (Basic Skills)
  • Uneven Bars
  • Low Beam
  • High Beam
  • Vaulting
  • Trampoline
  • Double Mini

This program develops advanced gymnastics skills, body strength, and knowledge of all competitive apparatus. Olympic events are taught in-depth.

Class Requirement:

Walkover; working on handspring

Children:

9-18 years old (grouped by age/skill)

Class Format:
  • Welcome
  • Warm-Ups/Basics/Stretching
  • Rotations through stations (15-20 min)
  • Conditioning
  • Cool Down and Culminating Activity
Events Covered:
  • Floor Exercise (Basic & Advanced)
  • Uneven Bars
  • Low Beam/Pommels
  • High Beam/Rings
  • Vaulting
  • Trampoline
  • Double Mini

Ideal for high school gymnasts or cheerleaders preparing for competitions.

This program develops tumbling skills for beginner to advanced participants. Designed for individuals/cheerleaders only.

Class Requirement:

No Skills

Children:

9-18 years old

Class Format:
  • Welcome
  • Warm-Ups/Basics/Stretching
  • Floor Exercise Stunting
  • Tumbling Rotations (events may change)
  • Conditioning
  • Cool Down and Culminating Activity
